CodeToast 🔥
What is this?

CodeToast is an AI-powered web app that brutally roasts developer portfolios, projects, and tech stacks — while also giving actual useful advice to improve them.

You paste your portfolio description, and the AI responds like a senior developer who’s tired of seeing “Netflix Clone using React” for the 500th time.

Funny? Yes.
Painful? Also yes.
Helpful? Surprisingly yes.

Why we built it

We were tired of:

portfolios that all look exactly the same
fake “10x developer” bios with zero real projects
generic feedback like “Looks good!” that helps nobody
people not knowing how recruiters actually judge portfolios

Most portfolio reviews are either too soft or too technical.
We wanted something entertaining enough to share with friends but useful enough to genuinely improve your work.

What makes it different

Most AI review tools act professional and polite.

CodeToast doesn’t.

It gives feedback with sharp, funny, senior-developer energy — then follows up with real actionable improvements instead of just insulting you and leaving.

Features include:

AI-generated portfolio roasts
Adjustable roast intensity — Mild, Medium, Savage
3 genuine improvement tips after every roast
Copy-to-clipboard support
Share directly to Twitter/X and LinkedIn
Fast and lightweight UI

The idea is simple:
make feedback memorable so developers actually improve.
